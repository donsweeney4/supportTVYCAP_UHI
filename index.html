What are the app's key features?

Our app helps you to collect temperature and humidity data, with key features including pairing with a remote temperature sensor, real-time temperature monitoring, and data sharing.

Yes, we take your privacy and data security very seriously. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
